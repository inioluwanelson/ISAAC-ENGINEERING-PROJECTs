Bush Clearing & Site Preparation

Location: Okwagbe, Delta State, Nigeria

ISAK ENGINEERING LIMITED executed comprehensive bush clearing using both mechanical slashers and manual labor to ensure complete vegetation removal. The work included uprooting shrubs, disposing of biomass responsibly, and leveling key areas in preparation for upcoming road and drainage construction. Environmental guidelines were followed to preserve surrounding ecosystems while ensuring safe access to the site.

Culvert Construction & Drainage Systems

Location: Kwale, Delta State, Nigeria

ISAK ENGINEERING LIMITED successfully executed the construction of reinforced concrete culverts to facilitate efficient water flow beneath access roads. The project included:

  • ✔ Excavation and formation of culvert beds
  • ✔ Fabrication of reinforced box culverts and headwalls
  • ✔ Laying of precast pipe culverts (as required)
  • ✔ Compaction and backfilling with lateritic material
  • ✔ Concrete finishing and erosion protection

The culvert system now ensures improved drainage, road durability, and enhanced access in previously flood-affected zones.
