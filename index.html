About Us

ISAK ENGINEERING LIMITED is a trusted civil and structural engineering company that provides expert services in road construction, erosion control, drainage systems, land reclamation, and much more.

We are committed to excellence, reliability, and innovation in every project we handle across Delta State and beyond.

Our Core Services

Road Design

Efficient layout, planning, and alignment of roads to optimize flow and safety.

Road Survey

Accurate topographic and route alignment surveys for design precision.

Road Construction

Complete road infrastructure construction using quality materials and methods.

Culvert Construction

Durable and efficient water passage solutions for road and site protection.

Drain Construction

Structured concrete or earth drains for effective floodwater control.

Erosion Control

Protecting land and structures from erosion through engineering techniques.

Drain Profiling

Site Supervision
